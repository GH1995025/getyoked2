About Idle Cafe Tycoon

Idle Cafe Tycoon is a relaxing simulation game where you can build and manage your very own cafe. Hire staff, decorate your space, serve customers, and grow your business empire one cup of coffee at a time.

Game Features

Gameplay

  • Build and customize your cafe
  • Manage staff and resources
  • Create new recipes and menu items
  • Upgrade equipment and facilities

Tips

  • Balance menu prices for maximum profit
  • Invest in staff training to improve service
  • Decorate your cafe to attract more customers
  • Check in regularly to collect idle earnings
